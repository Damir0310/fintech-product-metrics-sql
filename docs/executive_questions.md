# Executive Questions

This document lists high-level questions that product, growth, finance, and operations teams can explore using the SQL queries, synthetic data, and documentation in this repository.

## Growth and Acquisition

1. Which acquisition channels bring the highest-value users?
2. Which channels generate many signups but weak monetization?
3. Which channels have the strongest trial-to-paid conversion rate?
4. How do paid and organic channels differ in user quality?
5. Which countries are driving the largest share of new users?
6. Which country and channel combinations deserve deeper growth investment?
7. Are recent signup cohorts showing stronger activation than earlier cohorts?

## Revenue and Monetization

8. What is the monthly revenue trend?
9. How are MRR and ARR changing over time?
10. Which plans contribute the most revenue?
11. Which countries produce the highest revenue per user?
12. Which acquisition channels generate the most net revenue?
13. Are yearly plans healthier than monthly plans?
14. Which user segments have the highest lifetime revenue?
15. Is revenue growth coming from more paid users or higher revenue per user?

## Retention and Churn

16. Which plan has the lowest churn?
17. Which countries have the best retention?
18. Which acquisition channels have the highest churn?
19. What are the most common cancellation reasons?
20. Which users are most likely to churn?
21. Are newer cohorts retaining better or worse than older cohorts?
22. How much churn is offset by reactivation?
23. Which user segments should receive retention campaigns?

## Payments and Operations

24. How much revenue is affected by failed payments?
25. Which payment providers have the highest failure rate?
26. How quickly are failed payments recovered?
27. Which failed payment reasons are most common?
28. Do users with failed payments churn more often?
29. Are refunds materially affecting net revenue?
30. Which data quality checks should pass before reporting revenue, churn, or payment success rate?

## Strategic Decisions

31. Where should the company reduce paid acquisition spend?
32. Which markets appear most promising for deeper localization or growth effort?
33. Which lifecycle moments should product teams improve first: onboarding, trial expiration, payment failure, or cancellation?
34. Which metrics should be monitored weekly by product, growth, finance, and operations teams?
35. What combination of channel, country, plan, and payment behavior defines the healthiest users?
