# Business questions

The SQL library is organized around questions a fintech subscription product might revisit regularly.

## Growth and acquisition

1. How are daily and monthly signups changing?
2. Which countries contribute the largest share of users?
3. Which acquisition channels provide both scale and quality?
4. How does paid acquisition compare with organic acquisition?
5. Which channel-country combinations have high signup volume?

## Activation and monetization

6. What percentage of registered users starts a trial?
7. What percentage of trial users becomes paid?
8. Which channels have high signups but weak monetization?
9. Which countries have the strongest signup-to-paid conversion?
10. How long does it take users to move from signup to trial and payment?
11. Which plans are selected most often after a trial?

## Revenue

12. How much gross and net revenue is collected each month?
13. What MRR and ARR run rate do collected charges imply?
14. How does ARPU change over time?
15. Which plan contributes the most revenue?
16. Which channels and countries bring the highest-value users?
17. How much revenue is reversed through refunds?

## Retention and churn

18. What is the monthly gross churn rate?
19. Which signup cohorts retain product activity best?
20. Do yearly plans retain paid users better?
21. Which countries have the best paid retention?
22. Which acquisition channels have unusually high churn?
23. Why do users cancel, and how much MRR is associated with each reason?
24. How often do canceled users reactivate?

## Payments and lifecycle

25. What are payment success and failure rates by month?
26. Which providers and failure reasons create the most friction?
27. How much attempted value is exposed to failed payments?
28. What percentage of failed payments is recovered within seven days?
29. Are payment failures associated with later cancellation?
30. Which high-value or at-risk users merit deeper product research?
